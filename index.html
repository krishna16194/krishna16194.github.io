I'm a software engineer who likes turning fuzzy, manual problems into clean, automated tools. I care about the whole loop — parsing the input, doing the hard part well, and handing back something polished.

My recent work spans a document-translation app that outputs professionally formatted Word and PowerPoint files, an inventory management system, and a handful of web-based utilities.
